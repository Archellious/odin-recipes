Pancakes

Description:

In this recipe, we are going to make the fluffiest pancakes. Let's get it done!

Here is what you will need:

  • 2 Eggs
  • Two spoonful of sugar
  • 2 water glasses of flour
  • Vanilla powder
  • 1 and a half glasses of milk
  • Fruits of your liking!

Steps:

  1. First of all, get a big bowl.
  2. Now, crack both of your eggs into it.
  3. Add your sugar to the eggs
  4. Now start mixing them together.
  5. After you get a good mix, add the flour.
  6. And now goes in the milk!
  7. Start mixing the new mixture and add vanilla powder
  8. Start heating your pan and put very little oil on it.
  9. Pour your smooth mixture on the pan, as big or small as you want your pancakes to be!
  10. Cook them until they start to get holes appear and then flip them over.
  11. Once you cook the other side to your liking, get them off the pan to a plate.
  12. Decorate and add flavor with your favourite fruits, and most importantly; Enjoy!
